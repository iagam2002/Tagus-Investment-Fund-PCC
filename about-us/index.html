Welcome To Tagus Investment Fund PCC

Committed to Delivering Exceptional Value through Global Expertise and Tailored Investment Strategies.

  From  Idea  to  Impact —  Together.

Tagus is a Mauritius-based investment fund structured as a Protected Cell Company and licensed by the Financial Services Commission. We offer a secure, regulated platform for investors seeking tailored solutions across capital preservation, income generation, and long-term growth. With access to Indian and global markets, our diversified portfolios are designed to balance opportunity with disciplined risk management.

Our strategy targets returns above the risk-free rate through investments in equities, derivatives, infrastructure debt, and alternative assets. By leveraging arbitrage, hedging, and deep market insight, we aim to deliver consistent, risk-adjusted performance. At Tagus, we combine innovation, compliance, and investor focus to create enduring financial value.

Our Mission.

Our mission is to deliver superior investment solutions by combining disciplined strategies, expert market insight, and strong risk management. We focus on diversification, seizing market opportunities, and adapting to global trends to generate consistent returns and protect capital. Through transparency and excellence, we strive to build trust and drive long-term financial success for our investors.
